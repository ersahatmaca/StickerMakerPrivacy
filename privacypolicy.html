Privacy Policy for TinyArtist

Effective Date: [13/01/2024]
1. About TinyArtist

TinyArtist is an application designed to analyze children's drawings, offering art critique using OpenAI's GPT-4-Vision API. Our goal is to provide insightful and engaging feedback on your child's artistic creations.

2. Data Collection and Use

We prioritize your privacy and data security. TinyArtist does not collect any personal data from its users. All information you provide, including your child's name, birth date, and drawings, is stored locally on your device. However, for the art critique feature, we use OpenAI's GPT-4-Vision API, which involves processing the drawings on OpenAI's platform. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data.

3. No User Accounts

TinyArtist operates without user accounts. There is no need to sign up or log in to use our application. This means we do not collect email addresses, passwords, or any other account-related information.

4. Data Storage and Security

Since all data is stored on your device, you have complete control over it. We do not have access to this data, nor do we store it on any external servers. We recommend that you maintain your device's security to protect your data.

5. Children's Privacy

As our application is designed for children's artwork, we are committed to protecting the privacy of young users. However, we do not collect any data from the users, thus ensuring a secure environment for children.

6. Policy Updates

Any future changes to our privacy practices will be posted in this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
